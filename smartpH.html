Smart_pH: Universal pH Estimation via Artificial Neural Networks

Smart_pH is a smartphone-based colorimetric analysis system powered by Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) for fast, accurate, and universal estimation of pH values from indicator strips. The app integrates image processing, machine learning, and a multi-source dataset to ensure robust predictions under diverse lighting and camera conditions.

  • Platform: Android (Google Play Store)
  • Input: Image of pH indicator strip (captured or uploaded)
  • Output: Predicted pH value with visualization and accuracy indicator
  • Model: Artificial Neural Network (ANN) trained on multi-source RGB datasets

1. Capture or Upload Image

Open the app and either take a photo of your pH indicator strip using your camera or upload an existing image from your gallery.

2. Adjust Color Boxes
